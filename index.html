Simarpreet Kaur

Simi Chahal made her Punjabi film debut with the 2016 film Bambukat directed by Pankaj Batra and starred alongside Ammy Virk which was a success. Rabb da Radio and Chal Mera putt are some hit films. She impressed everyone with her beauty and acting. Chahal won a Filmfare Award (Punjabi) for Best Debut Actress in 2017 for her role in Bambukat, and, in 2018, won the Best Actress Critics Award at the PTC Punjabi Film Awards.
